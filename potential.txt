2. COMPANY THESIS (Bow would care)
===============================================================================
Problem: autonomous drone swarms are going to the front line (Firestorm et al.),
  but their comms are the soft target - jam or hack them and the swarm fails.
  There is almost no autonomous, ML-driven defense for swarm mesh comms today
  (existing AI-security players target web/IT, not UAS mesh).
Product: continuous, ML-driven resilience + intrusion detection for tactical
  mesh networks. Attaches to a real (or digital-twin) swarm, detects jamming/
  intrusion in real time, and keeps the network connected under attack.
Customer: defense / gov (DoD, allied forces), drone manufacturers, critical-
  infrastructure mesh (maritime, energy).
Why now: heavy battlefield jamming (e.g. Ukraine) + the rush to autonomous
  swarms = urgent, unmet need. DoD is actively funding autonomous cyber defense.
