Logistics & Cybersecurity (Software-only) Track
This software-only track gives teams the flexibility to explore defense problems through data, software, cybersecurity, and AI. Projects can focus on defense supply chains, contracting logistics, procurement, transportation, inventory risk, or mission-critical resource allocation. Teams may also choose to explore cybersecurity-focused problems such as intercepting radio messages, detecting suspicious activity, decoding secret messages, analyzing vulnerabilities, or modeling cyberattacks on defense systems. Teams are welcome to use any dataset or API they find useful, including but not limited to the example resources listed below.
Teams are not required to use these resources. These are only example datasets, APIs, and references that may be helpful.
